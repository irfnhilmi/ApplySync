Privacy Policy

ApplySync — Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

ApplySync stores the following data locally in your browser:

  • Your Google account email address (to display your connected account)
  • Your Google Sheets spreadsheet ID (to link back to your tracker)

This data never leaves your device except to communicate directly with Google's own APIs.

How your data is used

ApplySync uses your Google account exclusively to:

  • Create and write to a Google Sheet in your own Google Drive
  • Read job listing details from the current browser tab when you click "Auto-fill"

Your job application data is written directly to your personal Google Sheet. It is never sent to any third-party server.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party
  • We do not store your data on any external server
  • We do not track your browsing history or activity
  • We do not use your data for advertising or profiling

Third-party services

ApplySync communicates only with Google APIs (Sheets, Drive, OAuth2) on your behalf. Your use of Google services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
